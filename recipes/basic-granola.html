Easy Granola

Here is a super simple and easy-as-heck granola recipe that anyone can whip up and make in less than 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pinch of Cinnamon
  • 3 Cups - Large Flake Oatmeal
  • ¼ cup - Your choice of either Maple Syrup, Honey or Agave Nectar
  • A dash of Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • 4 tbsp - Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Cup Total - Extras of your choosing (nuts, raisins, chocolate chips, pretzels, etc)

Things you'll need:

  • Oven
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Oven Tray
  • Stir Stick
  • Measuring Cups

Instructions:

  1. Pre heat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Combine the oats, baking soda, and add-ins. Add the oil, honey, and mix well
  3. Use the Large Scoop to transfer the mixture into the oven pan (make sure to pack down the mixutre so it's tight).
  4. Bake for 14–16 minutes, or until the oats start to brown.
  5. Once baked, safely remove the pan and let it stand to cool of and harden for 20-30 minutes
  6. Once the granola and pan have cooled off remove the granola and place into your container of choosing for later enjoyment!
