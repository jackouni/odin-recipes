Fried Veggie Basics

I'm about to show you non-cookies (like myself) how easy it is to eat a healthy and tasty meal. Too simple...

Ingredients

  • Veggies of your choosing that'll fit in a frying pan (carrots, green beans, corn, tomatoe, onion, peppers, etc)
  • A tbsp of your favorite BBQ Sauce
  • Salt
  • Your choice of Butter, Margarine or cooking Oil

Things you'll need:

  • Stove Top
  • Frying Pan
  • Spactula

Instructions:

  1. Heat up stove top (I would recommend starting with your stove top dial just under the half-way point and then figure out later if you need more heat).
  2. Place the desired amount of oil/butter you want to cook with (less it better to start with and then increase if things are sticking to the pan). Then let that butter/oil heat briefly.
  3. Place your selected portion of veggies onto the pan.
  4. Cook your veggies to your desired doing (I personally like it when my veggies have some very slight burn to them)
  5. Once cooked, serve 'em and eat 'em.
