Jack's Waffles

Here is a super simple recipe for people who have a sweet tooth in the morning!

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon
  • Toaster Waffles
  • Maple Syrup (or a syrup of your choosing)
  • Butter or Margarine

Things you'll need:

  • Stove Top
  • Frying Pan
  • Butter Knife
  • Spatula

Instructions:

  1. Pre heat your stove top with your frying pan waiting.
  2. Take out the number of toaster waffles you want to make.
  3. Butter both sides of the waffle(s) with your butter knife.
  4. Place waffles in the frying pan (1 at a time or multiple if you can fit).
  5. Sprinkle a little your desired amount of cinnamon onto the tops of your waffles (I would start with a few pinches)
  6. From here you want to flip the waffles over and cook them to a golden brown on each side
  7. Once you have your waffles a golden brown, take them off, let them cool and then serve them with or without maple syrup
